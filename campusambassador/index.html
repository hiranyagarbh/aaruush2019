About Campus Ambassador


The Aaruush Campus Ambassador programme selects hard-working and enthusiastic students across campuses in India to play leadership roles and conduct and market events of the fest.

The candidate should have exemplary management and networking skills to engage batchmates in activities; organize events and work through a team of volunteers. An effective communicaton, the candidate must consistently keep-in-touch with Team Aaruush, providing relevant information to be able to best serve the college community.

Why Campus Ambassador?



Get Certified

Stand a chance to represent Aaruush in your college and collaborate, build and grow as a Campus Ambassador to certified for your work.

Organize events

Brainstorm on new ideas for publicity of events and bring them to reality by organising them to the best of your capabilities.

Leadership skills

Leading from the front and being the face of Aaruush in your respective campus will help inculcating exemplary leadership skills.

Ideate Building

To be the brains behind the scenes and collaborate in organising events will allow you to grow within a team environment.

Responsibility of a CA

REPRESENT AARUUSH IN YOUR COLLEGE

Be the face of Aaruush by organising and publicizing our events in your college on offline and online platform


IDEATION

Brainstorm on new events, artists and venues for the festival and experience your ideas come to life


COORDINATION AND PUBLIC RELATIONS

Collaborate with the team in organising on-ground events in your city along with managing public relations from our end



Prerequisites






1. Effective communication skills.
2. Exemplary knowledge about your college.
3. Contacts with students and faculty of your college. 
4. The ability to work hard and work smart.
5. Good time management skills.

Highlights

Musical Night 2018

As a part of the welcome to the freshers, Aaruush organises the Musical Night, to enthrall them with their favourite tunes and give them a time to remember. Last year, was no exception and saw a humoungous crowd sway to some foot-tapping music. The musical night is our way to welcome the freshers and give their college experience a memorable inception.

SRM RUN

With the aim of increasing awareness about clean water and sanitation, Aaruush took the initiative of organising the SRM RUN 4.0. The event bound the youth together for a cause and truly did justice to them being called the backbone of a developing nation. The youth came together for the 5.4km long run and pledged for improved quality of water and sanitation in the nation.

Tech Fair

The Tech Fair 2018 was a revolutionary evening that was filled with enthusiasm, excitement and inquisition as it rekindled fond memories of childhood and taught a lesson or two about the advancing technology. The events intertwined fond memories of our childhood with the ever changing levels of technology, leaving everyone with a memorable evening.

Carnival

True to its name, the Aaruush Carnival is fun, frolic and fiesta, served under one roof. It was one of the most joyous events of the year, with fun-filled activities that broke the monotony of everyday college life and came as a breather to everyone. It is that time of the year where exciting prizes are up for grabs and drool-worthy food is available at the stalls for everyone to make the most of.

Local Train

The supremely talented band, The Local Train, graced Aaruush'18. They had the audience up and pumping with their electrifying performances. Their melodious tunes were well complimented by the audience as they touched numerous hearts with their songs. The evening left the crowd with a handful of memories that'll last them a lifetime.

Varun Thakur

One of India's prominent comedians in the business, Varun Thakur lit up the comedy night of Aaruush'18. With relatable jokes and quality level comedy, Varun Thakur gave the crowd quite a show. He ensured that every single person in the crowd enjoyed the show and their stomachs hurt with all the laughter.

Informals

A breather away from all the technicality that the fest has to offer, there are also a number of informal activities, that aim at giving a fun and enjoyable time to the participants. A few of the informals that have been conducted over the last edition were Soap Football and Body Zorbing. The students were encouraged to indulge in these fun activities to enjoy and make the most of.
