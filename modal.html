T-Summit

SRM IST takes leaps with the idea fulfillment of social needs with innovative thinking. To accomplish this idea Technical Summit (T-Summit) brings to the table concepts and plans that contribute towards the betterment of the society and bridge the gap between technological advancement and social needs. A few esteemed colleges included IIT Kanpur, IIT Madras, IIT Hyderabad, NIT Trichy, NIT Nagpur, NIT Calicut, IIT Tirupati, NIT Raipur, VIT Vellore and Manipal University have been a part of this summit.
