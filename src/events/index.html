PUBG

Theme: Battlegrounds is a player versus player shooter game in which up to one hundred players fight in a battle royale, a type of large-scale last man standing deathmatch where players fight to remain the last alive.

Round 1: Shoot and collect
There will be various locations with different capacities. The participant will get any two locations from game of darts and he has to fill stuff according to his bagpack.

Round 2: Can you sell your bagpack
The candidate will sell the products acquired in the 1st round to a panel. The participants making the most amount of money will go the next round.

Round 3: The auction
There will be an auction of the no of team players, ammunition, first aid, backpacks, pain killer etc. Every gun will have their own rate of health damage, players may buy vests, helmets and other devices.

Round 4: Time to battle
The players will be asked to play one on one. After the opponent is dead they can pick up what he is left with but the capacity in their bag will always be a constraint. This process continues till there is a clear winner. Time will be ticking and after a certain amount of time spent in the individual battle; both participants will start losing some health. They can buy stuff using the fund retained in round 2 midway through the battle but not before.

THE WORLD WAR

THEME: Simulated World War in Modern Era

DESCRIPTION:
This event is about a simulated world war experience between different countries and region. Each participant representing their country must figure out ways to tackle the crisis given to them during the event.

Round 1: Redesigning Flag
Each participant will have to identify the given country based on given clues and then redesign the given country’s flag.

Round 2: Map Quest
Participant will have to assemble the jigsaw pieces of map to resemble the original map shown which will further lead them to another clue that would help the participant decipher a code message.

Round 3: Crisis Management
Delegates of each country must provide solutions for the given world without harming their country’s foreign policies and foreign relations. A series of questionnaire will be asked where they will have to answer in such a manner that the damage is least. Formation of alliances will also take place.

Round 4: World War
Participants would be given a huge world map in which all the crisis would be displayed from time to time. The delegates must figure out how will they solve that crisis for their country. With the backstory being provided the participant can raise their viewpoints and actions in the committee. Each action made by different countries will be recorded and will be constantly be altered in the common timeline.

IRON RULE

Round 1:

Big Dam Theory

The main event is based on construction of prototype of dam using thermocol and other materials to control the flow of water. In the event, proper dimensions for the dam and gates in the dam will be provided as constraints and the participants has to construct it within a given time limit. The dam built should be strong enough to withstand the pressure of water flowing through it.

Truss Mania

This event is based on the concepts of a truss. The participants will have to construct a truss of balsa wood of given dimensions and given specifications. The truss will be judged based on the load it can handle, feasibility and accuracy of the specifications of the truss.

Bridge-e-staan

This event is based on the concepts of a cable stayed bridge. The participants will have to construct a cable-stayed bridge of given dimensions and materials. The bridge will be judged based on the load it can handle, feasibility and accuracy of the specifications of the bridge.

PARKSHALA

The main event is based on construction of prototype of hydraulic car-parking system using ice cream sticks and other materials to make the structure. In the event, proper dimensions for the system and levels of floors in the system will be provided as constraints and the participants has to construct it within a given time limit. The system should be strong enough to withstand the weight of given toy car and can handle two floor system.
