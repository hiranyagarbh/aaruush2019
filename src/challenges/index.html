Hack Summit

Aaruush brings to you, Hack Summit, a 36 hour long Hackathon with the objective to give the students a platform to showcase their technical skills on a national level. Hackathons are the ultimate test of an engineerâ€™s skill and endurance. A true test of technical knowledge, creativity, time management, public speaking skills and problem-solving abilities of the participants.

Under Hack Summit we have 5 different challenges aimed at giving a multidisciplinary approach to conventional hackathons.

  • Smart Cities - In the SMART CITIES CHALLENGE participants can incorporate information and communication technologies (ICT) to enhance the quality and performance of urban services such as ENERGY, TRANSPORTATION UTILITIES ( water supply, garbage disposal etc) and COMMERCE in order to reduce resource consumption, wastage and overall costs. Participants may use any technology in order to develop a system which must enhance the quality of living of citizens through smart technology.
  • HealthCare - The HEALTHCARE CHALLENGE is a platform for developing products which can help doctors make more accurate diagnostics, and allowing them to reach more patients than ever before, by utilizing technologies like Machine Learning, AI and IoT. Participants can aim to develop ideas for actual products or prototypes in order to make a difference for patients as well as healthcare professionals around the globe.
  • FinTech and Cyber Security - FINANCIAL TECHNOLOGIES include any technological innovation in the financial sector, including advances in financial literacy, advice and education, as well as streamlining of wealth management, lending and borrowing, retail banking, fundraising, money transfers/ payments, investment management and more. CYBER SECURITY refers to the body of technologies, processes, and practices designed to protect networks, devices, programs, and data from attack, damage, or unauthorized access.
  • Media and Entertainment - The digital transformation of the media and entertainment industry has resulted in a dynamic and evolving ecosystem that has become increasingly complex and has introduced new challenges in the area of digital rights, advertising, augmented reality, virtual reality, mixed reality, etc. The MEDIA & ENTERTAINMENT challenge aims to identify innovative products that cater to the needs of the media industry.

Life Support Design Challenge

An emergency life-support system is a guardian angel, a protector, meant to be deployed immediately after natural disasters strike in order to ensure the safe sustenance of life for the crucial hours following the disaster.

Participants are given an opportunity to present their ideas and concepts of Life Support Structures and Systems.

Apart from the opportunity to present their ideas to a panel of experts in the field of design, disaster management and architecture participants stand a chance to win exciting cash prizes and also an opportunity to see their ideas come to life through the support of veterans in the field.

Autonomous Robot Challenge

In this event, participants are given a chance to show off their skills in the field of robotics. The aim is to develop an autonomous robot which can navigate its way through the arena and arrive at the end point, in the shortest duration possible. The participant may choose to take part in either the expert or master level.

Participants stand a chance to take home amazing cash prizes.

Revival of Rivers Challenge

Pollution of our water bodies is one of the biggest environmental crises we are facing today on our planet. Water is the most basic need for sustenance of life. There are various kinds of wastes that our water bodies are polluted with and the world of technology has seen many ideas that can improve the condition of our water bodies.

To solve this problem, we invite creative and young minds from all over the world to bring out ideas that can change the condition of our water bodies.

Participants of this challenge stand a chance to take away huge cash prizes and are also given an opportunity to present their ideas to a panel of experts from the industry. Exceptional ideas may even stand a chance to see their ideas come to life through the support of veterans in the field.
