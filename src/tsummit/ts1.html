T-Summit 1.0

The first edition of T-Summit was a unique and a new dais introduced on a national scale. It served as a vessel for the young & ingenious minds of the nation. It brought them together where they could focus all their intellect collectively and expound on the following agendas:

  • Energy & Sustainability
  • Defence & security
  • Frugal innovations
  • Joining Govt. Of India initiatives
  • Innovation culture
  • Joining Govt. Of India initiatives
  • Innovation culture
  • Renewable resources
  • Education of Underprivileged
  • Incorporating Quality Management in all Fests
  • Environment
  • Entrepreneurship Motivation
  • Improving the day to day lives of the people
  • Bridging gap between students & industrialists and improving quality management in all fests.

The summit was chaired by Mr. Atul Gurtu (Leader of the Indian Team at CERN, Geneva.), Dr. Narayana Rao, Dr. V.M. Ponniah, and Dr. A. Rathinam.

A few esteemed Colleges which attended the summit included; IIT Kanpur, IIT Madras, IIT Hyderabad, NIT Calicut, VIT Vellore & Manipal University.

The summit saw an astounding panacea on the discussed agendas. The committee ended with the new agendas for the next T-Summit been agreed upon. Furthermore, it highlighted how a cordial relationship between participating organizations was critical for the betterment of innovation & technology and for fostering the idea of innovation in the country.
