T-Summit 2.0

The second edition of the summit saw some of the brightest minds of the nation gracing the T-Summit with their presence. The theme for the T-Summit’17 was coined “Young Innovation”. The agendas for discussion at T-Summit 2.0 ranged from the following-

  • Projects and Published papers to increase innovation culture in the country.
  • Achieving Sustainable Development
  • Defence & Security
  • Joining Government of India Initiatives
  • Incorporating Quality Management in all Festivals.
  • Platform for Entrepreneurship.
  • Improving day to day lives of people with advancement of technology
  • Bridging gap between students and industrialists
  • Improving the quality of water bodies and possibilities of building green structures
  • Education of Underprivileged

The T-Summit’17 committee was chaired by Mr. Atul Gurtu (Leader of the Indian Team at CERN, Geneva). Among the other guests were Dr. Seshagiri Rao V (Former Associate Director, ISRO SDSC SHAR) and Ms. Neeta Verma (Director General of the National Informatics Centre).

A few esteemed Colleges which attended the summit included-IIT Madras, NIT Calicut, Amity University Rajasthan, NIT Raipur, NIT Delhi.
