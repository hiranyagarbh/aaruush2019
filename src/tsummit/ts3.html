T-Summit 3.0

The delegation of over 15 colleges through the country represented by the most passionate and technically inclined students resulted in mutual broadening of the perspectives.These young minds under the guidance of the many dignitaries from a vast diversity of fields learnt and understood what it means to innovate and implement and what it takes to break conventional barriers for wholesome growth and development.

  • Projects and Published Papers to increase the innovation Culture in the Country.
  • Improving the day to day lives of people through advancement in Technology.
  • Achieving Sustainable Development, with special emphasis on the Sustainable Development Goals.
  • Education of Underprivileged
  • Improving the quality of water bodies and possibilities of building green structures
  • Bridging the gap between students and industrialists
  • Defence and Security
  • Incorporating Quality management in all festivals.
  • Joining government of India Initiatives
  • Platform for Entrepreneurship motivation
  • Protecting & Promoting human health conditions
  • Raising human & institutional activities for climate change
  • Increasing student employment rate
  • Promoting sustainable agriculture & rural development
  • Incorporating critical analysis and criticism analysis in the framework of fests.

T-Summit ’18 was chaired by Mr. Atul Gurtu (Leader of the Indian Team at CERN, Geneva) Some of the other dignitaries’ present were Dr. R. K. Sinha (Former Director, Bhabha Atomic Research Centre), Dr. J. Daniel Chellappa (Indian Nuclear Scientist with Department of Atomic Energy), Dr. Rakesh Agarwal (Distinguished Professor of Chemical Engineering in Purdue University), Mr. Sandeep Sancheti.

A few esteemed colleges included NIT Trichy, NIT Nagpur, NIT Calicut, IIT Madras, IIT Tirupati, NIT Raipur.

The ideas envisioned by the T-Summit committee promises to set aside all the hurdles discussed from the nation’s path leading to a better tomorrow. It plans to achieve growth of the Technical Festivals in India through a forum of coherent discussion and deliberation by the brightest technical event management minds in the nation.
